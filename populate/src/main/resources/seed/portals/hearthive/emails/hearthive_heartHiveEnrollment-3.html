Here's what you need to know: 

  • Stay Informed: We'll notify you as we add relevant research studies to the portal. 

  • Your Data Privacy: Your data is safe with us. We won't use it for research without your consent. If you choose to take part in any research, we'll provide clear details about how your data will be used. 

  • Easily Join Studies: Joining a study is simple. Just complete an online consent form that explains all the study details. You're in control and can choose which studies to participate in. No obligations. 

  • Flexible Participation: If you ever change your mind about a study, you can withdraw at any time. It's easy – just log in and follow the withdrawal instructions. 

  • Get in Touch: If you have any questions or encounter technical issues, don't hesitate to email the Heart Hive team. We're here to help!
